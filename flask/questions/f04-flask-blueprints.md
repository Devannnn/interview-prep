# What is a Flask blueprint and why use it?

## Why recruiters ask this
- Tests whether you can scale a Flask codebase cleanly.
- Checks modularity and separation of responsibilities.

## Answer structure (keep it concise)
1. Define blueprint as a way to organize routes/components by domain.
2. Explain benefits: modularity, reuse, and cleaner app factory setup.
3. Show when it becomes necessary (larger teams/projects).
4. Mention impact on testing and maintainability.
